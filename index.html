About me

Hi! I am Roman, and this is my portfolio. Here you can find some information about me, my education and my skills.

I had graduated from Tel Hai Academic College, Israel in 2009 as a Software Developer. After that, I worked as a database administrator for a small company where I managed a local database for a company warehouse. Beside that, I worked as freelancer and did projects for clients. In 2017, I got Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics in Bar Ilan University, Israel, and after that I moved to Canada.

I enjoy to be a team player. The organization of the workplace and workflow play an important role for me. Over the years of working in freelance, I have learned to work with people, to understand their needs, and to help them to solve problems. Now I decided to change directions and do web development. I love to create something new, to give it form and functionality.
